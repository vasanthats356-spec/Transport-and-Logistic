FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our logistics services

What logistics services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of logistics services including road freight, sea freight, air freight, warehousing, and last-mile delivery solutions across India.

How can I track my shipment?

We provide real-time GPS tracking for all shipments. You will receive a unique tracking number that allows you to monitor your shipment's progress from pickup to delivery.

What are your typical delivery timelines?

Delivery timelines vary by service type: Express delivery takes 1-2 days, Standard delivery takes 3-5 days, and International shipping takes 5-10 days.

Do you offer customized logistics solutions?

Yes, we provide fully customized logistics solutions tailored to your specific business requirements. Our team will work with you to design the perfect solution.
