Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive logistics services including road freight, sea freight, air freight, warehousing, and distribution solutions across India and internationally.

How do I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using our real-time GPS tracking system. We provide a unique tracking number for every shipment.

What is your delivery timeline?

Delivery timelines vary based on the service selected. Express delivery takes 1-2 days, standard takes 3-5 days, and international shipping takes 5-10 days.

Yes, we offer fully customized logistics solutions tailored to your specific business needs and requirements.
